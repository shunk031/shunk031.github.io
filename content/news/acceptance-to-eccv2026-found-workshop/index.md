Our workshop proposal for the 2nd FOUND Workshop, ["FOUND: Foundation Data for Industrial Tech Transfer @ ECCV 2026"](https://eccv2026-found-workshop.limitlab.xyz/), has been accepted at ECCV 2026. The workshop will be held on the morning of September 9, 2026, in Malmö, Sweden, in conjunction with ECCV 2026.

> [More Information about the Workshop](/post/eccv2026-found-workshop/)

This workshop builds on the FOUND Workshop accepted at ICCV 2025 and continues the discussion on Foundation Data, domain-grounded datasets, and practical tech transfer for foundation models. The ECCV 2026 edition places a particular focus on Physical AI and World Models, along with robustness under domain shift, out-of-distribution conditions, and long-tail distributions.
